ABOUT ETH-ILORIN

Ethilorin is a community for ethereum enthusiasts and developers in ilorin to connect, collaborate, and learn more about the lastest development and best practices in the Ethereum ecosystem, We believe in transparency, security, and community ownership. Our mission is to make blockchain accessible to everyone by developing tools, protocols, and smart contracts that empower users to control their digital identity and assets.
